About us
What we believe in

Support local fishermen

In all countries that we work with, we make sure to support local families. It is one of the best ways our business can create long lasting impact.

Building long lasting relationships with everyone we work with

We believe in working together for mutual benefit and growth. It can take time for us to be fully committed to working with someone, but once we have built a relationship, we always look out for them.

Honesty and trust

Honesty and trust are key ingredients to a successful business relationship. We stand by our values and expect the people we work with to do the same.
